Burger

Description

One of the best and easy to finish foods. Popular almost everwhere in the world, you can never go wrong with a burger on a weekend!

Ingredients

  • Two pounds extra-lean ground beef
  • One ounce package dry onion soup mix
  • One egg, lightly beaten
  • Two teaspoons hot pepper sauce
  • Two teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • One-fourth teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Three-fourth cup rolled oats

Steps

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, onion soup mix, egg, hot sauce and oats. Shape into 6 patties.
  3. Grill patties over medium high heat for 10 to 20 minutes, or to desired doneness.
