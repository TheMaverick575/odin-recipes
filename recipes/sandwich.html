Sandwich

Description

Sandwhiches are everyone's favourte thing, Just the leisure of grabbing one and eating it while doing whatever you want

Ingredients

  • Two slices American cheese
  • Two slices white bread, toasted
  • Three slices deli-style sliced turkey breast
  • Two tablespoons Russian salad dressing

Steps

  1. Place American cheese onto one slice of toasted bread. Microwave 15 to 20 seconds on High until cheese has melted. Place turkey onto melted cheese. Spread Russian dressing onto one side of the remaining slice of toasted bread. Place on top of turkey, dressing-side down to create a sandwich.
